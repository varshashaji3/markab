Family Office

We believe that true prosperity is reflected in what we are able to pass on to our families and our children. Everything we do at Markab – for our clients’ families and our own – is driven by this principle.

We strive to give our clients the peace of mind that comes with a trusted partner, working alongside you and your loved ones, to ensure that your wealth is carefully looked after for generations to come. We are dedicated to contributing to our clients’ future aspirations through the Markab Multi-family office platform.

Our Goal


Our primary goal is to provide a sense of security and peace of mind to our clients by becoming their trusted partners. Working alongside you and your loved ones, we ensure your wealth is meticulously managed and preserved for the coming generations. With our multi-family office platform, we collaborate to curate financial strategies that not only address your present financial needs but also embrace the enduring aspirations of your family.


At Markab Capital, we are dedicated to contributing to our clients’ future aspirations. Our commitment extends beyond the immediate and focuses on long-term vision and sustainability. Through our multi-family office platform, we seek to be a guiding force in realizing and fostering the dreams and ambitions you hold for your family’s future. Our dedication is rooted in ensuring that your legacy remains an enduring testament to the values and successes you wish to impart.
